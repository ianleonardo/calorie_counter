Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Health Coach ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our calorie counting and nutrition analysis application.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect the following information from your Google account:

  • Name — to personalise your experience
  • Email address — to identify your account
  • Profile picture — to display in the application header

We also process food images you upload for nutritional analysis. These images are sent to a third-party AI service for analysis and are not permanently stored by us.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and identify you within the application
  • To provide calorie and nutrition analysis of your meals
  • To display your profile information in the app

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as necessary to provide the core service (e.g., sending food images to an AI service for analysis).

5. Data Storage & Security

Your session data is stored securely on the server. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Uploaded images are processed in real time and are not retained after analysis is complete.

6. Your Rights

You may log out at any time to end your session and clear your stored session data. Since we rely on Google Sign-In, you can also revoke access to this application from your Google Account permissions page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application.
